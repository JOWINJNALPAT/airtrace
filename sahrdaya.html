Sahrdaya College of Engineering & Technology - Leading Engineering Institution Kerala

Sahrdaya College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) has achieved remarkable recognition as one of the top 15 engineering colleges in Kerala, moving up from rank 24 to rank 15 in 2025. This autonomous engineering college maintains NAAC B+ accreditation and NBA accreditation.

Located in Kodakara, Thrissur, Kerala, with 1800+ students, 180+ faculty, 90%+ placement rate. Programs: Computer Science Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Biotechnology, Biomedical Engineering, AI & ML, Data Science, Cyber Security. Affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University.
